About Us

An in-depth description of Ankara Fiesta: "Ankara Fiesta is an annual fashion extravaganza that brings together designers, industry professionals, and fashion enthusiasts to celebrate the rich heritage of African-inspired fashion. Since its inception in [year], Ankara Fiesta has evolved into a premier event, showcasing cutting-edge designs, promoting emerging talents, and fostering innovation within the fashion industry.
Ankara Fiesta is convened by Aderinsola Ogunleye, a passionate advocate for fashion and creativity. With a deep appreciation for African textiles and designs, Aderinsola Ogunleye has dedicated themselves to organizing this remarkable event, bringing together diverse talents and pushing the boundaries of fashion."
